# Introduction

Welcome to the 'WhatchaDoin Bot', an exciting new Discord bot that brings the spontaneity and community feel of BeReal into your Discord server. Developed by the BeReal meets Discord team, this bot encourages users to share and interact through real-time snapshots of their daily activities. It's all about creating a genuine, inclusive community space where everyone can connect and share moments of their lives. With user-friendly moderation to maintain a respectful environment, 'WhatchaDoin Bot' is set to redefine your Discord experience.
